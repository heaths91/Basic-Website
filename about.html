About

I am 26 years old, living in Tacoma. Currently studying in the Geospatial Technology Master's program at the University of Washington Tacoma. I am very passionate about helping people with disabilities within the community, and hope to be able to do that with GIS and web mapping.

Prior to enrolling in this program, I worked for Google Maps, and the Kittitas County Planning Office. I graduated from Central Washington University, receiving my BS in Geography specializing in GIS. This website will serve as a place to showcase my work throughout the next year and beyond.

In July of 2015 I suffered a spinal cord injury, that left me paralyzed from the chest down (T4 incomplete, if you're into that type of thing). I currently play basketball for the Tacoma Titans wheelchair basketball team, and volunteer as a peer counselor for other who have suffered spinal cord injuries.
